No scripts. No crews. Just real athletes, real stories.

Nike partnered with Cinebody to put the creative power directly in the hands of athletes and influencers. Instead of flying in production teams, Cinebody's platform guided creators through shot lists from their own gyms, trails, and neighborhoods — capturing the kind of raw, first-person content that resonates on social.

The result was a library of authentic influencer stories that felt native to every platform. From morning workout routines to daily training diaries, each piece of content carried the credibility of a real athlete's voice — no polish needed, just honest storytelling at scale.
