Pop-Up Power

Product drops. Live activations. Content captured in real time.

Nike's pop-up events move fast — a new shoe drops, a community run launches, and the energy is gone by morning. Cinebody was on the ground at every activation, arming Nike's own teams with guided shot lists so they could capture the raw energy of each moment without waiting on a production crew.

From limited-edition product launches to surprise run club meetups, every event became a content engine. Footage flowed into the platform in real time, giving Nike's marketing team fresh, authentic assets they could push to social within hours — not weeks.
