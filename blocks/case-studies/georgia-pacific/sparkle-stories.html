Real Stories

No scripts. No studios. Just real customers, real kitchens.

Sparkle needed content that felt like a recommendation from a friend, not a pitch from a brand. Cinebody put the camera in the hands of real customers and let them show how Sparkle fits into their everyday lives -- messy counters, busy mornings, and all the in-between moments that make a paper towel brand actually relatable.

The result was a library of creator-driven content that outperformed studio shoots on every metric. Authentic demos, honest testimonials, and real-world use cases gave Sparkle a voice that connected with audiences across social, digital, and retail media channels.
