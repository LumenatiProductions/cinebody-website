Intellectual Property

Protected by patents.

The Cinebody platform and other Cinebody products are covered by the following U.S. and International Patents and Patent Applications.

U.S. Patents

Granted
  • U.S. Patent No. 9,824,477 Remote Video Capture Direction Covers the core technology that allows administrators to remotely control camera settings (resolution, FPS, orientation) on a filmer's mobile device through a web-based interface, without requiring an app download.
  • U.S. Patent No. 10,410,389 Real-Time Video Synchronization Covers the real-time cloud sync technology that automatically uploads footage from a filmer's device to the administrator's dashboard the moment recording stops, eliminating manual file transfers.
  • U.S. Patent No. 9,722,656 Guided Shot List Technology Covers the patented shot list system that delivers creative direction, technical specifications, and guided prompts directly to a filmer's screen during recording, ensuring on-brand content capture at scale.

U.S. Patent Applications

Pending
  • Application No. 1516.100.00US Multi-Source Content Aggregation Methods for collecting and organizing video content from multiple distributed sources into a unified dashboard.
  • Application No. 1516.100.01US Browser-Based Camera Control Systems for controlling device camera functionality through a standard web browser without native app installation.
  • Application No. 1516.100.02US Distributed Production Coordination Methods for coordinating simultaneous video production across multiple geographic locations through a single platform.
