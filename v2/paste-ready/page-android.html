Knowledge Base

Android browser camera.

Learn how to film directly to a Cinebody project via the Android browser camera. Access your shot list, record clips, and upload footage automatically.

How it works

01

Open your invite link

Tap the link sent by your project admin. It opens directly in your Android browser — no app download required.

02

Review your shot list

See exactly what you need to film: resolution, orientation, creative prompts, and technical specs are all displayed on screen.

03

Start filming

Tap record. Cinebody controls your camera settings remotely — FPS, resolution, and orientation are locked to the project specs.

04

Footage syncs automatically

The moment you stop recording, your clip uploads to the project dashboard. No manual transfers, no waiting.

Quick Tips
  • Use a well-lit environment for best results
  • Hold your phone steady — use both hands or a tripod
  • Check your internet connection before filming
