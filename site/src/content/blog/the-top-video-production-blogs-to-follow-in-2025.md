<h4><strong>Stay Up on the Trends</strong></h4>
<p>The world of video production is more dynamic now than it has ever been. Besides the writers &amp; actors strikes that have halted all new television and movie production, the advancement in technologies are astonishing. It goes without saying, staying up to date with these latest trends, techniques, and technologies is crucial.&nbsp;</p>
<p>And there’s no better way to stay in the know with all things video production than by following the top video production blogs. They not only provide insights into the industry's best practices, but also can offer inspiration for your next project.</p>
<p>So to make your life simple, we’ve compiled our top video production blogs that you should be following in 2025.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h3><a href="https://nofilmschool.com/"><strong>No Film School</strong></a></h3>
<p>No Film School is a go to for all things video production. It’s a comprehensive resource for filmmakers of all levels, offering a wide range of content from equipment reviews and industry news to interviews with top filmmakers. The blog's DIY approach encourages independent filmmaking, and provides valuable insights into the world of video production.</p>
<h3><a href="https://www.premiumbeat.com/blog"><strong>The Beat</strong></a></h3>
<p>PremiumBeat by Shutterstock is already known as a go-to resource for affordable stock music, but did you know that its blog, “The Beat,” provides high-quality content on video editing, motion design, color grading, and more? It's a great resource for both beginners and professionals looking for tips, tutorials, and industry news.</p>
<h3><a href="https://wistia.com/learn"><strong>Wistia</strong></a></h3>
<p>Wistia is one of the top video marketing and hosting companies around, and its blog is a treasure trove of video marketing tips and tricks. It offers in-depth guides on various aspects of video production, including scripting, shooting, editing, and promoting videos. If you're looking to leverage video content for marketing, this blog is a must-follow. Not to mention, their original series <a href="https://wistia.com/learn/show-news/introducing-one-ten-one-hundred">“One, Ten, One Hundred”</a> with the production company, Sandwich, is both fascinating and widely entertaining.</p>
<h3><a href="https://www.videomaker.com/"><strong>Videomaker</strong></a></h3>
<p>Videomaker is one of the oldest and most respected video production blogs around. It offers a wealth of information on all aspects of video production, from pre-production to post-production. The blog also provides reviews of the latest equipment and software, helping you stay up-to-date on the current trends.</p>
<h3><a href="https://blog.frame.io/"><strong>Frame.io Insider</strong></a></h3>
<p>Frame.io Insider offers deep dives into the craft of filmmaking and video production. It features interviews with industry professionals, behind-the-scenes looks at major productions, and comprehensive guides on various aspects of the production process.</p>
<h3><a href="https://www.redsharknews.com/"><strong>RedShark News</strong></a></h3>
<p>RedShark News is a leading source of news, features, and reviews in the world of video production and filmmaking. It covers a wide range of topics, including cameras, post-production, VR, and broadcasting. It’s definitely structured more as a news source than a blog, but we’ve included it in this list because it is one of the best resources for new innovations and trends.</p>
<h3><a href="http://philipbloom.net/blog/"><strong>Philip Bloom</strong></a></h3>
<p>Philip Bloom is a world-renowned filmmaker who shares his knowledge and experience on his blog. It's a great resource for in-depth camera reviews, behind-the-scenes looks at his projects, and tips on filmmaking techniques.</p>
<h3><a href="https://www.provideocoalition.com/"><strong>ProVideo Coalition</strong></a></h3>
<p>ProVideo Coalition brings together the industry's best writers, bloggers, and video gurus to one platform. It offers a wide range of content, including news, reviews, tutorials, and thought leadership articles.</p>
<h3><a href="https://www.cinema5d.com/"><strong>Cinema5D</strong></a></h3>
<p>Cinema5D is a leading blog for digital filmmaking news, reviews, and tutorials. It's a great resource for staying updated with the latest camera gear and filmmaking techniques.</p>
<h3><a href="https://www.cinebody.com/blog"><strong>Cinebody</strong></a></h3>
<p>Of course, we had to include our blog in the list. With remote video production becoming more and more of a go-to in the world of video content creation, the Cinebody blog provides a wealth of industry trends, tips and tricks, and other insights into producing video content with normal, everyday people vs professionals.</p>
<p>Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting out in video production, these blogs offer a wealth of knowledge and inspiration. By following these top video production blogs, you'll stay updated with the latest trends and techniques in the industry.</p>
