Year One

What we learned.
What we fixed.

Where we started

The early deliverables didn't land. We changed direction.

We opened with social content cuts that weren't at the quality level either of us wanted. Coordinating on-camera talent added friction that slowed everything down. We recognized it quickly, owned it, and pivoted rather than pushing through work that didn't represent either brand.

What we figured out

Sashco's team handles social well. That's not where we add value.

The realization: your in-house team is already strong on social content. We add value when we show up with a plan, a camera, and a full production day and execute work that's genuinely hard to do without a dedicated team behind it.

What clicked

Showing up on-site with a plan. Every time.

The HR and culture shoots proved it. When we arrived at Sashco HQ with scripted concepts and a structured shoot day, the work was strong. That's the model for Year 2, applied to the channel that actually needs it most.

The structural problem

Monthly retainers put the burden in the wrong place.

A monthly deliverable model requires your team to be ready every month. When priorities shift, months slip. Year 2 changes the structure so that doesn't happen.

The Focus
YouTube is where DIYers search.
That's where Sashco needs to show up.
From Google to AI search, the people asking "how do I fix this?" need to find you first. Year 2 is built entirely around that goal.

The Structure

Not a retainer.
An annual agreement.

One contract. A fixed production calendar. We drive it. Sashco shows up for shoot days and approves content. That's the full ask.

01

We set and drive the calendar

You approve the quarterly plan once. After that, we execute: shoots, edits, delivery, without needing to chase approvals or wait on bandwidth that isn't there.

02

One shoot day produces 6 to 8 deliverables

HQ shoot days are batched. We arrive with scripted concepts, camera setup, and a full shoot plan. One visit. Maximum output.

03

Focused agenda check-ins, not open-ended calls

Each check-in has a specific purpose: confirm which products to feature next, lock SKUs, align on the upcoming shoot. We respect your calendar.

04

Your only pre-work: confirm SKUs and mockup boards

Before each HQ shoot we send a specific list of what we need. You confirm the products and have the boards ready. We handle everything else from scripting to final delivery.
